SPLASH OF LIFE: The Ebb and Flow of Time

Description: Splash of Life is a meditative piece of art that symbolizes the quick pace and blur at which humans are living their lives these days. With its calm and light blue background, and the ripples that slowly come and go with time, this artwork encourages viewers to take a moment and observe the world around them with patience and intention to truly appreciate the beauty and value of the world that we live in. Only when we slow down and intentionally interact with the world around us can we discover our significant yet also insignificant role in the grand scheme of things. Then, only by acknowledging this can one come to peace. By engaging with Splash of Life, the viewers can become more mindful of the present. Not only does it serve as a momentary escape from everyday life, but it also invites viewers to reflect on their life. This artwork acts as an anchor that stabilizes the mind of the viewers and provides a chance to experience a calm and mindful state of being in the midst of chaos. By making a conscious effort to engage with the artwork, one can create a space for themselves to reflect, gain a deeper appreciation for the beauty amidst the chaos and find inner peace. Overall, with its subtle yet impactful presence, Splash of Life encourages us to take a step back and live in the present moment.
